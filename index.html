Martin Satrio, Ph.D.

Meteorological Radar Scientist, Rainmaker Technology Corporation, Norman, OK

Welcome! I am a Meteorological Radar Scientist on the research team at Rainmaker where I design and optimize radar-based precipitation estimation and visualization systems to improve cloud seeding strategies and support sustainable water resource management. I previously completed my postdoc at the Cooperative Institute for Severe and High-Impact Weather Research and Operations (CIWRO). Additionally, I have previously earned my Ph.D., M. S., and B. S. in Meteorology as well as my B. A. in Mathematics all from the University of Oklahoma. My published work has focused on using research and operational radar data to investigate severe weather, especially relating to supercell and tornado dynamics.
