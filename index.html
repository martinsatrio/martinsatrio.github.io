Welcome

Researcher in Severe Weather, Radar, and Supercell/Tornado Dynamics

Welcome! I am a Post-Doctoral Research Associate at the Cooperative Institute for Severe and High-Impact Weather Research and Operations (CIWRO) at the University of Oklahoma. I have previously earned my Ph.D., M. S., and B. S. in Meteorology as well as my B. A. in Mathematics all from the University of Oklahoma. My main area of research is using research and operational radar data to investigate severe weather, especially relating to supercell and tornado dynamics.
